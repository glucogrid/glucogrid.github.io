What exactly is Glucogrid?

Glucogrid was primarily made for researching diabetes and spreading awareness about it. However,it's than that:it aims to make diabetic people feel less alone in their diabetes journey. We will be posting an article weekly, keeping everyone up to date about the most relevant diabetes information.
